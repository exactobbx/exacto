Ethan “Exacto” Scott

Ethan Scott, known as “Exacto” on stage, is a professional beatboxer, teacher, and painter. Ethan has performed in over 250 shows in New York and internationally, 20+ comedy festivals, and corporate workshops for top name companies around the world.

Exacto teaches lessons and workshops for children and adults, offers private acoustic concerts for parties and events, plays with Sofar Sounds, and performs every Saturday night with his Hip-Hop Improv Comedy Team “North Coast.""
